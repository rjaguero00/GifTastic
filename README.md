# GifTastic

Live Demo: https://rjaguero00.github.io/GifTastic/


Movie Star GIFs!
Click a Actor to see the current top 10 popular giphy's or search for actor of your choice. Click on the still image to play or stop playing. To add an actor to the list, enter Actors' name of your choice into the textbox and click 'Find that Actor'. The new actor can then be accessed by clicking the actors name on the top of the page.
